My Projects

CeraWorks

The CeraWorks Ceramic Services App is a convenient solution for car owners to schedule and manage appointments for professional car detailing services. With our app, users can create an account, read or leave reviews, and easily schedule from a range of service options on Calendly. The app streamlines the booking process, saving time and effort for both customers and service providers. Experience the convenience of scheduling and managing car detailing appointments with the CeraWorks Ceramic Services App.

Craft and Cocktail

Craft and Cocktail is a responsive web application that generates random cocktails along with their recipe and instructions for how to make them. You can save your favorites to come back to later. It is great for giving you new ideas on what to order on a night out, or allowing you to stay in and save money by making your drinks at home!

Moon Dollars

The Moon Dollars Application is for the coffee lover with a creative side. Moon Dollars allows a user to create their own custom drink recipes and then save them for ease of access at a future time. They are able to add as many drink combinations as they like, name them and save them to their favorite drinks. The user is shown categories such as type of coffee, flavor, etc with various options that they can choose so that customization is easy and straightforward.
